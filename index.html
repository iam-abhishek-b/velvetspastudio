Our Philosophy

Wellness, elevated to an art form.

Velvet Spa & Studio was born from a simple belief — that rest is not a luxury, but a ritual. Every visit begins the moment you arrive: a warm welcome drink, a quiet consultation, and a space designed to slow your breath.

Our therapists are trained in authentic Thai, Balinese and Ayurvedic traditions, working with organic oils, warm herbal potlis and heated stones. Private rooms, steam and couple suites make every visit entirely your own.
